Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GlobalTalk as your Skype alternative

How is GlobalTalk different from Skype?

GlobalTalk offers up to 80% lower rates when calling international numbers compared to Skype. We also provide superior call quality, dedicated 24/7 customer support, and a simplified user experience designed specifically for international calling. Unlike Skype, we focus exclusively on providing the best international calling experience without bloating our app with features you don't need.

Which countries can I call with GlobalTalk?

GlobalTalk allows you to call international numbers in over 230 countries and territories worldwide. This includes both mobile phones and landlines in major countries like the USA, Canada, UK, Australia, Germany, India, China, Brazil, Mexico, and many more. Our coverage is comprehensive, ensuring you can connect with anyone, anywhere.

Do I need to switch phone numbers to use GlobalTalk?

Not at all! GlobalTalk works alongside your existing phone service. You don't need to port your number or make any changes to your current phone setup. Simply download the app, create an account, add credit, and start making international calls immediately. You can even choose to display your regular phone number as caller ID when making calls through GlobalTalk.

Can I use GlobalTalk on multiple devices?

Yes! Your GlobalTalk account works seamlessly across all your devices. Install our app on iOS, Android, Windows, and Mac to make and receive calls from anywhere. Your contacts, call history, and credit automatically sync across all devices, and you can even transfer an active call from one device to another without disruption.

Do you offer a free trial for new users?

Absolutely! New GlobalTalk users receive a 7-day free trial of our Global Explorer plan, which includes 600 minutes to 60+ countries. This gives you plenty of time to experience the superior quality and reliability of our service when calling international numbers. No credit card is required to start your free trial - just download the app and sign up.
